Coach

You're a man or a woman who wants to help teach women to program? We can use you! Don't worry, you don't have to be a developer. As long as you meet these requirements, you are a candidate.

  • Have some working knowledge of development environments like text editors, browser dev tools, etc.
  • Are able to complete the curriculum before the event. Coaches who do not do this will not be allowed to participate as a coach on the day of the workshop.
  • Have and will bring their own laptop.
  • Are willing to adhere to the Techlahoma Code of Conduct.
  • Interview with the organizer or co-organizer before the workshop.
